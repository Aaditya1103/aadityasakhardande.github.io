About Me

Hi there! I’m Aaditya Sakhardande, a Robotics Engineer pursuing a Master’s degree in Robotics and Autonomous Systems. My passion for robotics stems from a deep curiosity about intelligent systems, automation, and how machines can interact with and adapt to the world around them. This drive has led me to explore cutting-edge technologies that push the boundaries of robotics.
Throughout my journey, I’ve worked on exciting projects, from developing autonomous robots for navigation to implementing advanced signal processing techniques for real-world applications. I’ve tackled challenges in motion planning, kinematics, and robotic perception, leveraging AI and machine learning to enhance robotic capabilities. One of my key interests is integrating Large Language Models with robotics, exploring new ways to make robots more interactive and intuitive.
I have hands-on experience with tools like ROS, Python, MATLAB, and C++, and I enjoy bridging the gap between hardware and software to build robust, intelligent systems. Collaboration is at the core of my work—I thrive in environments where brainstorming, problem-solving, and hands-on prototyping lead to innovative solutions.
When I’m not working on robotics, you’ll find me keeping up with the latest advancements in AI, contributing to projects, or discussing the future of automation. I’m always eager to learn, experiment, and apply my skills to solve meaningful problems.
Thanks for stopping by my portfolio—I’m excited to share my work with you and look forward to connecting!
