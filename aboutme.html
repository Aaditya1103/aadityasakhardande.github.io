Hi there! I’m Aaditya Sakhardande, a Robotics Engineer with a Master’s degree in Robotics and Autonomous Systems.

My journey into robotics started with a deep fascination for intelligent systems and automation—how machines can perceive, adapt, and interact with the world. Over time, this curiosity has evolved into a passion for building innovative robotic solutions that push the boundaries of technology.

I've had the opportunity to work on a range of exciting projects, from designing autonomous robotic systems and solving complex motion planning challenges to integrating AI-driven perception for enhanced decision-making. Whether it's developing forward and inverse kinematics models, working with robotic arms like the MyCobot Pro 600, or optimizing control algorithms, I thrive in environments where hardware and software come together to solve real-world problems.

I have hands-on experience with ROS, Python, MATLAB, and C++, along with a strong foundation in robot kinematics, signal processing, and machine learning. I enjoy the iterative process of designing, testing, and refining systems to make them more efficient, intelligent, and adaptable. Collaboration is at the heart of my work—I believe the best solutions come from brainstorming, problem-solving, and sharing knowledge with a team.

Beyond robotics, I’m constantly exploring advancements in AI, automation, and cybersecurity. Whether it’s diving into research, contributing to open-source projects, or discussing the future of intelligent systems, I’m always eager to learn and grow.

Thanks for stopping by—I look forward to sharing my work and connecting with like-minded innovators!
